The Design Process

Data > Define > Design

Reading the above line at once or separately have the same implications for me.

Since I've started questioning a design solution, challenging assumptions about what is the best for consumers, observing their behaviours and developing empathy for the user and their final benefit, from that moment I knew that I was into something more complex than creating a well-presented design for my client.

Consequently, UX became of my interest and design thinking is the fundamental framework underpinning UX where empathise, define, ideate, prototype and test is the process constantly repeated for each micro or macro project starting with an user-centred sketching, prototyping and testing of ideas.

In essence, knowing the user needs and wants will entirely drive the process towards best outcomes in design benefiting customers and businesses, and this is possible following the simple statement: "Data define design".
